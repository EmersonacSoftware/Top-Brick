Top Brick Hall Of Complaints!

As mentioned in our privacy policy, the Top Brick Community enjoys a high level of transparency between the audience and the hosts. As such, petty and laughable complaints will be made public here, as they were received, along with the story behind it. We do not post your personal data, but we do keep usernames public as to confirm authenticity.

In April/May 2023, Emersonac Reviews met Brickfleet on Instagram and became friends over memes. Upon asking for advice, Brickfleet advised Emersonac to follow trends and memes to try and get a big break in the community, which led to some success. They collaborated and helped each other out on multiple occasions.

In July/August, Brickfleet’s account was hacked by an Elon Musk crypto bot, and Emersonac unfollowed him out of caution. When Brickfleet returned, he didn't respond to his messages and changed his content style. In September, Emersonac used a trending Markiplier green screen for a reel and changed their profile picture to showcase some new glasses he bought, unaware that Brickfleet had done the same.

On the 7th of October, Brickfleet accused Emersonac of plagiarism, despite Emersonac providing evidence of independent work. Other creators made the issue public, and Emersonac defended themselves by showing file creation logs, as well as the green screen pack he downloaded the Markiplier overlay from. Brickfleet, annoyed by the public defense, blocked Emersonac out of pettyness on social media, accusing him of altering EXIF data and using past drama with content creators as justification. Brickfleet also proceeded to spread false information about Emersonac to multiple content creators in the LEGO Instagram space.

Brickfleet still holds a grudge to Emersonac to this day, going as far as to kick him from MayDayFigs' Discord upon sight, with no justification. We have tried to contact him to fix ties but to no avail.
