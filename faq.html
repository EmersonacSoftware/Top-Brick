Q2: Why are the episodes voice-only?

The episodes are voice only because it is much simpler to record just audio. Since we are from 3 different timezones, it would be very, VERY hard to coordinate ourselves, and half of our members don't even have a proper recording studio.

Q3: When do you record?

We record at 6 AM GMT, every Sunday, on a private Discord server.

Q4: What are the requirements to be a guest on TB?

Be a LEGO content creator and have a decently large following. Please check our Request A Guest section for more details.

Q5: What do you use to edit?

We use OBS for recording, Audacity for sound editing, DaVinci Resolve for video editing, and paint.net for thumbnails and other media.
