Emersonac Reviews, Gundom, DB26E and GalaxieDragonX are four fans of LEGO just trying to make it on the Internets. Separately, they are nothing. But together, they are...slightly less nothing.

In each episode, these four amigos dive headfirst into the world of LEGO and beyond. Whether they're debating upcoming LEGO sets, news, LEGO-related shows, random shit from the Internet, making tasteless jokes, or discussing just how much effort they're actually putting into this damn podcast, one thing is for sure - your time is about to be wasted, every goddamn Saturday!

But that's not all! Sometimes, they also trick some poor saps into wasting their time as their special guests! Keep an eye out for all sorts of AFOL and TFOL users who've been forced to come on the podcast!

So join the Top Brick group on their magical journey. And by magical journey, we mean, trek through complete and utter insanity. Because when these four motherfuckers get together, anything (and we mean, ANYTHING) can happen. This... is the Top Brick Podcast.
